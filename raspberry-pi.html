Raspberry Pi

Raspberry Pi is a series of small single-board computers developed in the United Kingdom by the Raspberry Pi Foundation to promote the teaching of basic computer science in schools and developing countries.

With its low cost, versatility, and ease of use, Raspberry Pi has become a popular platform for DIY projects, home automation, and Internet of Things (IoT) applications.

Learn more about Raspberry Pi at https://www.raspberrypi.org/.
